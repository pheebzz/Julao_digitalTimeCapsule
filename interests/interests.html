Check out some of the artists who inspire me

Yayoi Kusama

Yayoi Kusama is a legend in contemporary art, minimalism, and abstract expressionism. She's best known for her infinite polka dot installations. I'm inspired by how she has made her deep-rooted personal experiences into honest, memorable, and authentic artwork.

Reina Koyano

Reina Koyano's "Sole Fatale" illustrations depict reinterpretations of sneaker silhouettes into powerful women. From a career standpoint she motivates me to draw inspiration from what I enjoy during my art and design progression.

Toshiyuki Inoko

Toshiyuki Inoko is the founder of teamLab Art Collective, a collaborative group of artists in animation, architecuture and other various technology mediums to create art through digital technology. The art exhibitions in Japan introduce Interconnected Reality, which utilizes large physical spaces where people can interact with the digital art. Their exhibitions give me color inspiration through their vibrant and striking illusions.
