My Top 5 Ramen Restaurants in the Bay

Hokkaido Ramen Santouka
This is my favorite ramen restaurant and is worth the drive to San Jose in my opinion. Their broth light yet rich in flavor, plus the location inside a great Japanese market.
Marufuku Ramen
This ramen spot has many chain locations in the US and Japan. The Japantown location has been one of my go-to places when I'm around the area.
Ramen dojo
I like eating spicy food and this ramen spot serves delicious spicy flavored ramen. The entire menu only serves spicy broth dishes.
Santa Ramen
This restaurant is a nostalgic ramen spot because I've gone with family. The owner also owns both Ramen Dojo and Ramen Parlor.
Ramen Parlor
I enjoy eating here even though it gets busy quick. This place has unique broths like Lobster garlic and serves really good appetizers.
